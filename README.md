# Worlde

This mod adds the 26 English letters to minecraft, 10 numbers and capacibility to spanish players with the **Ñ** and even a -

## Blocks in the mod
- 26 English Letters
- Letter ñ
- 10 Numbers
- one -

## 4 colors avaible (Gray, yellow, red and green)

Every letter, number and symbol is available in the four colors and **Each color has his own brush to paint blocks with it's color**

## Available locations
in this moment the mod is located to En_Us and Es_Mx
